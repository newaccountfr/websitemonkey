About

Monkey Clicker is a fun and addictive clicker game coded by a 9th grade student by the name of Charles Oberlander. Monkey Clicker is a game coded in pure ES6 Javascript, with a couple libraries which follow: Bootstrap, W3CSS, and jQuery. He hopes that one day this website will become popular and people all around the world can pass time by playing this.

Info

The first section, "Cursor Count," is where you click the image of a monkey to get score. The "upgrades" section allows you to buy cursors in exchange for a higher cursor rate per click. The third section, "Autoclickers," clicks for you, but at an exponential cost. Have a fun time playing this game!
